🦷 Voluntary Dental Coverage (Guardian PPO)

Core Benefits Summary (PPO)

Calendar Year Deductible $0 / person (No Deductible)
Preventive Care (Cleanings, Exams) 100% Covered
Basic Care (Fillings, Root Canals) 80% Covered
Major Care (Crowns, Bridges) 50% Covered
Annual Benefit Maximum $2,000 / person

Orthodontia & Special Rules

  • Orthodontia Coverage (Kids to age 19):
    • Teachers, Act 93, Confidential: 50% Co-insurance up to a $1,000 Lifetime Maximum.
    • Support Staff / Transportation: Orthodontia is Not Covered under Kit 2.
  • Maximum Rollover: If you submit at least one claim and remain under the $800 threshold, Guardian rolls over $400 (plus an extra $200 if you use only in-network dentists) into a rollover account (MRA) capped at $1,500.
  • Support Staff Note: Support Staff (both 10 & 12 Month) are ineligible for the voluntary dental premium plan.
👁️ Voluntary Vision Coverage (EyeMed Insight PPO)

Exam & Glasses Copays

Routine Eye Exam $10 Copay ($0 at PLUS Providers)
Single Vision Lenses $25 Copay
Bifocal Lenses $25 Copay
Trifocal Lenses $25 Copay
Progressive Lenses (Standard) $85 Copay

Frame & Contact Allowances

  • Frame Allowance: $130 Allowance in-network ($180 at PLUS Providers like Pearle Vision / Target Optical). You get a 20% discount on any remaining balance over the allowance.
  • Contact Lenses (in lieu of glasses):
    • Disposable Contacts: $0 Copay and $125 Allowance in-network (balance over $125 is covered by a 15% discount).
    • Conventional Contacts: $0 Copay and $125 Allowance in-network (15% discount on balance).
  • Exam Frequency: Once every plan year for children, once every 2 plan years for adults. Lenses/frames also follow this frequency.
💊 Prescription Drug Coverage (Capital Rx)

Prescription drug coverage is automatically included with all CBSD medical plans. You do not need to register for it as an add-on, and there are no extra monthly premiums.

Standard Copays (30-Day Retail)

  • Tier 1 (Generic): $10 Copay
  • Tier 2 (Preferred Brand): $25 Copay
  • Tier 3 (Non-Preferred Brand): $40 Copay
  • Tier 4 (Specialty): $100 Copay

Mail Order Tiers (90-Day Supply)

  • Tier 1 (Generic): $20 Copay
  • Tier 2 (Preferred Brand): $50 Copay
  • Tier 3 (Non-Preferred): $80 Copay
